Everyday Life Hacks

Banana Hacks

These hacks are going to be filled with fruit love, so lets slow down the ripening process of our Bananas by wrapping a piece of plastic cling wrap around the crown of the banana bunch (the part where all the stems connect) which in turn gives us a longer life. The same can be said about leaving them in a paper bag that can actually speed up the rippening process.

Strawberry Hacks

Yet again we have another fruit hack larking in the shadows awaiting its turn for fruit glory, this time around the process will be completely different by using vinegar which is known for its antibacterial properties, The method goes as such, soak your berries for 5 mins in a mixture of vinegar and water, next rinse your berrries and let them dry before storing them in the fridge. Boom! you've just increased the life span of your fruit friends.

Relaxtion Hacks

We now dive into the world of mindfullness as it has become a new tool for the many to relax, destress, and quite the mind from the constant chatter you experience on a day to day basis that goes on in your mind. There has been lots of research done to show that just taking 5 - 10 mins in your day or however many times you'd prefer
Lets begin, sit in a confortable sitting position. Now take deep breaths in followed along with a slow exhale and we repeat this for 3 rounds to ground ourselves in the moment. Breath meditation is on of the simplest techniques out there but is the base structure of the practice, observe the breath as in goes in and out. There will be a string of thoughts that come up but dont worry about them, let them bubble up as you draw back your attention to the breath, just being with no judgements of yourself. These thought the arise is a natural thing as the mind slowly lets go of thoughts that are coming and going which is a natural thing but we dont attach ourselves to them but just observe them around realign ourselves back to the breath. As the mediation comes to an end you let go of the attention to the breath and just observe how you are feeling now compared to the past moment. We then take deep breath in and slowly exhale, we do this for another 3 rounds and now open your eyes.
There we go a quick but simple way to destress and relax in a matter of mins.
